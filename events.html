Events

Scintillating Moments. Priceless Memories.
  • filter_1Hall Day

    Hall Day is a very special event for each individual of the hall.Every VSite celebrates the hall day with great enthusiasm and since hall days of different halls are celebrated on seperate days, friends from other halls can also be invited to have a great evening. Hall day is accompanied by delicious food items, DJ Night and other awesome events. Hall day lives in our memory for all our life.

    Date : During march
  • filter_2Vidyasagar Jayanti

    Vidyasagar Jayanti is celebrated on 26th of september, the birth anniversary of Ishwarchandra Vidyasagar (the ocean of knowledge) the key figure of bengali Renaissance. Various events are organised in honour of the Ishwarchandra Vidyasagar like Debates, Drama, Dance and what not! A grand dinner is also organised

    Date : 26th September
  • filter_3Illumination

    Illumination acts as an event of enlightening the boarders and producing a spark in them during diwali time. The spark which makes every boarder give their all physical and mental effort for the hall.The Enthusiasm shown by VSites leads to a very lovely creation of bright designs using diyas showcasing, IITians can also act as good designers.There is a fierce competion between halls to create the best Illuminati design as it also a part of the Social And Cultural GC.

    Date : During Diwali
  • filter_4Rangoli

    Rangoli according to it's definition is an art form from native India in which patterns are created on the floor in living rooms or courtyards using materials such as colored rice, dry flour, colored sand or flower petals. A large amount of creativity is used by the participants to create a mind numbing rangoli design which can be either complex, elegant or both. No person can ever forget the memory of beautiful rangolis created by us, VSites

    Date : During Diwali
  • filter_5Vidyasagar Premier League (VSPL)

    Vidyasagar Premier League is a unique tradition in VS Hall, where students from VS Hall, and few other halls make their own teams, and come to have a friendly competition amongst themselves. The event is a visual treat for all, with humorous commentary by senior people of the hall, and decent prizes for all. The mess workers also play, with encouragement from all the VSites.

    Date : Around October
  • filter_6Udbhav

    Udbhav In this present scenario, where everyone is busy in competing with each other, we often lose the environment of a healthy and friendly competition.To promote the spirit of unity, sportsmanship and brotherhood, Vidyasagar Hall of Residence organises Udbhav, a friendly tournament-"Just play. Have fun. Enjoy the game." Entire KGP is invited to take part and win laurels in cricket and volleyball championship.

    Date : During March
